Data Sources

See what is behind {{ site.name }}

Transportation

The place's transport convenience factor is determined by the time it would take you to reach important hubs

Grocery Shopping

Walking distance to grocery stores, and their business hours determine how well a place scores on this factor

District Safety

Even though Berlin is nice and safe, crime-rate data of the district is factored in from statistics published by Berlin Police

Quietness

We make use of the noise-pollution report generously provided by the State Department of Urban Development

Broadband Network

Availability of a fast internet connection is provided based on a research done by Technologiestiftung Berlin

More

We would love to hear more ideas about extra data sources on hello@locationscore.berlin
