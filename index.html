Locations in the city have scores, depending what is close to them.
This tool explains what are the pros and cons of living in a certain place

Always up-to-date

LocationScore is a living, breathing measure of a place's civil infrastructure. Our data sources are being regularly updated, which is reflected in the absolute score value.

Compare your options

Add two or more places, and see how do they score between themselves. Individual Factors reflect what are the benefits of a given Place.

Leverage Open Data

Our formula uses Open Data, provided by Berlin city administration, which ensures the LocationScore is credible and representative.

Meaningful for You

Every Factor that contributes to LocationScore can be assigned more weight, to reflect you very own LocationScore, making this a perfect tool for educated decisions on renting, buying or investing in Real Estate.
